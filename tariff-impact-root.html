U.S. Tariff Economic Impact Simulator

Core Economic Indicators

Import Volume:

IM = 3.4T × (1 - 0.6 × ΔT)

Export Volume:

EX = 2.5T × (1 - 0.2 × ΔT)

Total Trade:

Total = IM + EX

GDP Growth:

ΔGDP = 2.1% + (-0.03 × ΔT)

Inflation Rate (CPI):

CPI = 2.8% + (0.25 × ΔT)

Business & Household

Tariff Revenue:

Revenue = Import × ΔT

USD/CNY Exchange:

ΔRate = -0.2% × ΔT

Corporate Profits:

Profit = 100 × (1 + 2 × ΔGDP)

S&P 500 Index:

S&P500 = Baseline × [1 + (GDP - CPI) × -1.2]

Household Income:

Income = Baseline × (1 - Inflation)

Consumption Capacity:

Consumption = Income × (1 + -0.3 × ΔT)

Consumption Growth Rate:

ΔConsumption = %ΔIncome + Elasticity

Investment Growth Rate:

Investment = f(Confidence, ΔGDP)

USD Exchange Rate Change:

ΔFX = f(Tariff Shock)
